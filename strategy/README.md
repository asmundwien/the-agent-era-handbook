# Strategy Playbooks

> **These are standalone strategy documents, not part of the core handbook methodology.** They cover business strategy topics (go-to-market, SaaS defensibility) that complement the handbook but are a separate body of work.

The core handbook (in `core/`) covers how humans and agents work together. These playbooks cover how to build and position products in the agent era.

| Document | Topic |
|---|---|
| [GTM & Distribution](gtm-developer-tools.md) | Launch playbooks, distribution channels, content strategy, distribution as defensible asset |
| [SaaS Defensibility](saas-defensibility.md) | Agent-era SaaS strategy, defensibility frameworks, pricing models |

These documents are not loaded into agent context and are not part of the pushback protocol.
